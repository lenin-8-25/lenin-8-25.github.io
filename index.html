About Me

I recently graduated with a B. Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Dr BR Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar. I'm proud to have been ranked among the top 12 students in my branch. My background is a mix of hardware and software, with a strong foundation in digital system design and full-stack development.

On the hardware side, I've designed and implemented projects like a 4-bit Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU) using Verilog and Xilinx Vivado and a Multi-Input Multi-Output Finite State Machine (FSM) for turnstile control. I also have experience with the I2C protocol for efficient data transfer between integrated circuits.

I also have experience as a Frontend Developer Intern at MetaCraftlab, where I gained proficiency in web development and version control. One of my key projects was building a full-stack marketplace, "F2C," for farmers to sell directly to consumers. I used React.js for the frontend, Express.js for the backend, and MongoDB as the database.

I'm passionate about creating efficient and scalable solutions, and I'm excited to apply my skills in digital design, embedded systems, or full-stack development to contribute to innovative projects.
